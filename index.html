About Aigle Consult

Aigle Consult is a consulting agency based in Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of the Congo. We support students wishing to pursue their studies in Europe with professional, transparent, and efficient guidance.

Through our partnerships with several European universities, we facilitate access to recognized programs and assist students in realizing their international academic projects.

Professionalism · Transparency · Student Success

Our Services

Academic Guidance

Personalized guidance to help students choose universities and study programs aligned with their academic background and career goals.

Application Assistance

Full support throughout the application process, including university applications, registration files, and administrative procedures.

Partner Universities

Guidance to universities in France, Belgium, Greece, Romania, and Northern Cyprus through established academic partnerships.
